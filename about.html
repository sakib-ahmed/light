STORY

Our mission is simple, to support underprivileged children around Bangladesh to have a better chance in education and in life. Specially to help prevent child labour and empower young people in developing nations to fulfil their potential through education, vocational training and life skills. So therefore, our fundraising helps give those most in need the very best opportunities to overcome barriers. And we do it with a smile!
